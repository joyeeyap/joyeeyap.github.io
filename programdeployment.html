Environmental Services Leadership Training

Business Need: Achieve our business financial goals, 2030 carbon neutral objective, and increase retention and employee satisfaction scores around development.



Solution: Developed a 6-month leadership training series for people leaders to enhance their executive leadership skills and technical acumen. Created framework to scale across departments.

Developing Talent Pipeline for Clinical Engineering Skills

Business Need: Talent shortage in biomedical technician clinical engineering roles. Time to fill positions is 45% higher than average IT roles. Unfilled roles pose great operational risk for overburdened teams.



Solution: Curated a 18-week hybrid upskilling training, to build an internal talent pipeline of employees with clinical engineering skills, during a hiring freeze.

Talent Pipeline Program Management

Business Need: Increasing demand for engineering and technical talent with shifting workforce goals. Need to attract and retain early-career and transitioning professionals with relevant skills.



Solution: Managed a graduate rotation program and launched a military internship to build an internal engineering talent pipeline, reducing time-to-fill for critical roles.

Women in Tech Leadership Series

Business Need: Women are underrepresented in technology roles and executive leadership. Increase access, visibility, and support for women in tech education programs.



Solution: Developed Women in Tech Leadership Series designed to build a strong pipeline of diverse, future-ready leaders, increase retention of high-potential women in tech, and support organization DEI goals.

Global Business Immersion Program Management

Business Need: Organizations need leaders who can navigate complex global enviornments, collaborate across cultures, and respond to international business challenges.



Solution: Hosted a global business immersion program to offer individuals first hand experience in international business context, strengthen their strategic thinking, cultural competence, and ability to lead across borders.
