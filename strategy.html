Strategic Partnerships for Business Success

Business Need: Ensure our workforce is equipped with the most relevant skills. Design a comprehensive learning curriculum that aligns with industry demands and fills existing skill gaps, as identified by the MSIS advisory board.



Solution: Develop new corporate partnerships to provide students with new learning opportunities ranging from company visits, hackathons, leadership seminars, and more. Filled existing skill gaps providing learning opportunities in the areas of cyber, cloud computer, artificial intelligence, etc.

Talent Strategy and Leadership Alignment

Business Need: Improve retention metrics and build pipeline of early-career engineering talent.



Solution: Create a talent committee of 40 executives to lead recruitment and retention efforts, serve as an advisory board, exchange best practices, and receive consultation on talent development initiatives.
